COBRA CODING

What We Are

We are a group of incoming high school juniors with experience in coding and python. We are interested in giving more opportunities for middle schoolers and incoming freshman to help lay the foundation for their future coding experiences. We believe that coding can be a fun and educational experience all in one and that is what we aim to teach through this camp! The camp will go through learning the basics of python and exploring concepts through fun projects!
